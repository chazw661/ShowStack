## Shape-class → autocomplete-source mapping (the decision for GAP-11.1)

Per Phase 10 D-04 ("same endpoint reused by PORT-03 without re-implementation") + Phase 10 D-05 (SystemProcessor excluded — no canonical label field). Phase 10's RESEARCH.md decision (carried into 11-CONTEXT.md §H) is that the endpoint is project-scoped but otherwise broadcasts all 9 sources. **CONTEXT.md and RESEARCH.md never specified a per-shape mapping — that decision is required to close GAP-11.1.**

Defensible mapping below — derived from each shape's equipment-model source on the Django side:

| Shape `type` | Equipment record (picker scope) | Autocomplete sources |
|---|---|---|
| `showstack.Console` | `Console` (planner/models.py) | `Console Input`, `Console Aux Out` |
| `showstack.Device` | `Device` | `Device Input`, `Device Output` |
| `showstack.Amp` | `Amp` | `Amp Channel` |
| `showstack.Processor` | `P1Processor` / `GalaxyProcessor` (not `SystemProcessor` — Phase 10 D-05) | `P1 Input`, `P1 Output`, `Galaxy Input`, `Galaxy Output` |
| `showstack.SpeakerArray` | (no I/O label catalog — pure output transducer) | **fall through to all 9** |
| `showstack.CommBeltPack` | (no I/O label catalog — comm endpoint) | **fall through to all 9** |
| `showstack.Generic` | (no equipment record by design) | **fall through to all 9** |

Rationale: SpeakerArray, CommBeltPack, and Generic don't have a labeled-channel catalog of their own. Restricting them to "no suggestions" would punish the engineer for picking a flexible shape; broadcasting all 9 sources is the same behavior they get for the connector circuit-label today. Document this decision in the plan SUMMARY so a future v2.4+ revisit can see why three shape types share the broadcast path.

Backend allowlist-only behavior: unknown `shape_class` values (typo, future shape not yet wired) also fall through to all 9. The endpoint must never 500 on bad input.
