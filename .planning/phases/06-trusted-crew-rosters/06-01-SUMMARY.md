### Task 1 — Model classes in planner/models.py

Three new model classes inserted at lines 752–843 (immediately after `ProjectAccessRequest`, before the `#-----Console Model----` section):

- **`Crew`** (line 752): Owner-scoped named roster. `owner = FK(settings.AUTH_USER_MODEL, related_name='owned_crews')`, `name = CharField(100)`, `created_at`/`updated_at`. `unique_together = (('owner', 'name'),)`. Per D-02.

- **`CrewMember`** (line 773): Single-table polymorphic row. `crew = FK(Crew, CASCADE)`, `user = FK(AUTH_USER_MODEL, null=True, related_name='crew_memberships')`, `email = EmailField(null=True)`, `default_role`, `added_at`. Three DB constraints:
  - `CheckConstraint(condition=Q(user set, email null) | Q(user null, email set), name='crewmember_user_xor_email')` — enforces XOR at DB level (D-15, T-06-01-01)
  - `UniqueConstraint(fields=['crew','user'], condition=Q(user__isnull=False), name='uniq_crewmember_crew_user')` — partial unique (T-06-01-03)
  - `UniqueConstraint(fields=['crew','email'], condition=Q(email__isnull=False), name='uniq_crewmember_crew_email')` — Postgres NULL-not-distinct workaround (Pitfall 1, T-06-01-02)

- **`CrewProjectAdd`** (line 820): Audit link table. `crew = FK(Crew, related_name='project_adds')`, `project = FK(Project, related_name='crew_adds')`, `added_at`. `unique_together = (('crew', 'project'),)`. Per D-09.

Also added at line 2: `from django.db.models import CheckConstraint, UniqueConstraint, Q` (was not present in the file).
